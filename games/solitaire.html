About Solitaire

Solitaire, also known as Klondike Solitaire, is a patience card game. The goal is to build four foundation piles, one for each suit, in ascending order from Ace to King.

How to Play: Drag cards to build sequences. Move cards to foundation piles starting with Ace and ending with King!
